Welcome to TwitterOnTap! On this site, you can perform some basic sentiment analysis operations. Firstly, you can search a topic on Twitter by providing a keyword to query and then see what the sentiment is towards that topic in the Twitterverse. Furthermore, you can choose a Twitter topic check what its sentiment history is. Finally you can also utilize your own personal sentiment analysis tool by providing a sentence and checking the sentiment that it expresses. You can find out more about this site on the About page . Thanks for checking out TwitterOnTap.
